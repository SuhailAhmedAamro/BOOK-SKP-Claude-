# Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**. 

This book is designed to take you from a beginner to an expert in building intelligent physical systems.

### What you will learn:
* **The Nervous System:** ROS 2 and Robot Communication.
* **The Digital Twin:** High-fidelity simulation in NVIDIA Isaac Sim.
* **The Brain:** Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for decision making.
